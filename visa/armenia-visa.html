Armenia Visa for Indian Travelers: Complete Guide

Planning to explore Yerevan, Lake Sevan, or the majestic monasteries of Armenia? Indian travelers require a visa to enter Armenia.

Skyway Tours and Travels ensures a smooth and hassle-free Armenia visa application process for your travel convenience.

Do Indian Citizens Need a Visa for Armenia?

Yes, Indian passport holders require a visa to enter Armenia. E-visas and embassy applications are accepted.

Types of Armenia Visas for Indian Travelers

1. Tourist Visa

  • Best for: Tourism and sightseeing
  • Validity: Usually up to 120 days (single or multiple entry)
  • Processing Time: 3–5 working days for e-visa

2. Business Visa

  • Best for: Business meetings, conferences, or trade visits
  • Validity: Up to 180 days (single/multiple entry)
  • Requirement: Invitation letter from Armenian company or organization

Armenia Visa Processing Time

  • E-Visa: 3–5 working days
  • Embassy Visa: 5–7 working days

Skyway Tip: Apply at least 7–10 days before your travel date to avoid delays.

Main Attractions and Things to Do in Armenia

1. Yerevan

  • Explore Republic Square and the history-rich Cascade Complex.
  • Visit museums like the History Museum of Armenia and Genocide Memorial.
  • Enjoy Armenian cuisine and cafes in the city center.

2. Lake Sevan

  • Relax at Sevan Peninsula beaches.
  • Visit the historic Sevanavank Monastery and enjoy panoramic views.

3. Garni & Geghard

  • Tour the Garni Temple, a Greco-Roman landmark.
  • Explore the Geghard Monastery, carved into cliffs.

4. Dilijan & Lake Parz

  • Enjoy scenic hiking trails and forested landscapes.
  • Visit the tranquil Lake Parz for boating and nature walks.

5. Khor Virap & Areni

  • See the iconic Khor Virap Monastery with Mount Ararat backdrop.
  • Explore Areni village, famous for wine tasting and local culture.

Important Notes

  • Passport must be valid for at least 6 months from the date of entry.
  • Carry a printed copy of your visa approval and supporting documents.
  • Visa type must match your travel purpose (tourist or business).

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. Can Indian citizens apply for an Armenia visa online?

Yes, e-visa applications are accepted for Indian travelers.

2. How long is an Armenia tourist visa valid?

Typically up to 120 days, single or multiple entry.

3. What documents are required for an Armenia visa?

Passport valid for at least 6 months, completed application form, photograph, and invitation letter if applicable.

4. How long does it take to process an Armenia visa?

E-visa: 3–5 working days; Embassy visa: 5–7 working days.

5. Can I extend my Armenia visa?

Extensions are generally possible through Armenian migration authorities under special circumstances.

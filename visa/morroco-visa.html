Morocco Visa for Indian Travelers: Complete Guide

From vibrant souks to the Sahara Desert, Morocco is a destination full of culture and adventure. Indian travelers require a visa to visit Morocco.

Skyway Tours and Travels ensures a smooth Morocco visa application process so you can enjoy your trip without any hassles.

Do Indian Citizens Need a Visa for Morocco?

Yes, Indian passport holders require a visa to enter Morocco. Tourist and business visas are available depending on the purpose of travel.

Types of Morocco Visas for Indian Travelers

1. Tourist Visa

  • Best for: Leisure travel and sightseeing
  • Validity: Typically up to 90 days
  • Processing Time: 5–7 working days

2. Business Visa

  • Best for: Business meetings or conferences
  • Validity: Up to 90 days
  • Requirement: Invitation from a Moroccan company or organization

Main Attractions and Things to Do in Morocco

1. Marrakech

  • Explore the bustling Medina, souks, and the famous Jemaa el-Fnaa square.
  • Visit Bahia Palace and Majorelle Garden.

2. Fes

  • Discover the UNESCO-listed Fes Medina, traditional tanneries, and historic architecture.

3. Chefchaouen

  • Stroll through the iconic blue-painted streets and enjoy the scenic Rif Mountains.

4. Sahara Desert

  • Go on camel treks, experience desert camping, and witness breathtaking sunsets.

5. Casablanca

  • Visit the Hassan II Mosque, the Corniche, and modern city attractions.

6. Essaouira

  • Enjoy coastal beaches, seafood, and the UNESCO-listed old town.

Important Notes

  • Passport must be valid for at least 6 months from entry.
  • Carry a printed copy of your visa application and supporting documents.
  • Ensure visa type matches your travel purpose (tourist/business).

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. Can Indian citizens apply for a Morocco visa online?

Currently, Indian travelers must apply for a Morocco visa at the Moroccan consulate or through authorized visa agencies.

2. How long is a Morocco tourist visa valid?

Typically up to 90 days for single entry.

3. What documents are required?

Passport valid for at least 6 months, completed visa application form, photographs, travel itinerary, and hotel bookings.

4. How long does visa processing take?

Approximately 5–7 working days, depending on the consulate and application completeness.

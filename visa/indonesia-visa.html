Indonesia Visa for Indian Travelers: Complete Guide

From Bali’s beaches to Jakarta’s bustling streets, Indonesia offers a rich cultural and natural experience. Indian travelers require a visa to enter Indonesia.

Skyway Tours and Travels makes the Indonesia visa application process easy and efficient, so you can focus on your adventure.

Do Indian Citizens Need a Visa for Indonesia?

Yes, Indian passport holders require a visa to enter Indonesia. Eligible travelers may apply for an e-visa or visa on arrival for short stays.

Types of Indonesia Visas for Indian Travelers

1. Tourist Visa

  • Best for: Leisure travel and sightseeing
  • Validity: Up to 30 days (single entry)
  • Processing Time: 3–5 working days for e-visa

2. Business Visa

  • Best for: Business meetings or conferences
  • Validity: Up to 60 days (single or multiple entry)
  • Requirement: Invitation from an Indonesian company

Indonesia Visa Processing Time

  • E-Visa: 3–5 working days
  • Visa on Arrival: Instant at designated airports

Skyway Tip: Apply at least 7 days before travel to avoid delays.

Main Attractions and Things to Do in Indonesia

1. Bali

  • Relax on Kuta and Seminyak beaches.
  • Explore Ubud’s rice terraces and cultural hubs.
  • Visit temples like Tanah Lot and Uluwatu.

2. Jakarta

  • Experience the nightlife and shopping in the capital city.
  • Visit the National Monument (Monas) and museums.

3. Yogyakarta

  • Explore Borobudur Temple and Prambanan Temple.
  • Experience traditional arts, batik, and local markets.

4. Komodo & Lombok

  • See Komodo dragons in Komodo National Park.
  • Enjoy diving, snorkeling, and hiking in Lombok.

5. Sumatra & Borneo

  • Adventure travel: orangutans, volcano trekking, and wildlife tours.
  • Explore Lake Toba and tropical rainforests.

Important Notes

  • Passport must be valid for at least 6 months from entry.
  • Carry a printed copy of your visa or approval letter.
  • Visa type should match travel purpose (tourist/business).

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. Can Indian citizens get an Indonesia visa online?

Yes, e-visas are available, or you can get visa on arrival at select airports.

2. How long is a tourist visa valid?

Up to 30 days (single entry).

3. What documents are required?

Passport valid for at least 6 months, visa application form, photograph, and hotel or travel itinerary.

4. How long does visa processing take?

E-Visa: 3–5 working days; Visa on Arrival: immediate at airports.

5. Can I extend my Indonesia visa?

Yes, extensions are possible through Indonesian immigration offices for eligible visa types.

Kenya Visa for Indian Travelers: Complete Guide

Kenya is a land of stunning landscapes, wildlife safaris, and rich culture. Indian travelers require a visa to visit Kenya.

Skyway Tours and Travels ensures a smooth Kenya visa process so you can focus on exploring the beauty of this East African gem.

Do Indian Citizens Need a Visa for Kenya?

Yes, Indian passport holders require a visa to enter Kenya. E-visa options are available for tourists and business travelers.

Types of Kenya Visas for Indian Travelers

1. Tourist Visa

  • Best for: Safari trips and sightseeing
  • Validity: Typically up to 90 days
  • Processing Time: 3–5 working days for e-visa

2. Business Visa

  • Best for: Meetings, conferences, and business travel
  • Validity: Up to 90 days
  • Requirement: Invitation from a Kenyan company or organization

Kenya Visa Processing Time

  • E-Visa: 3–5 working days
  • Visa on Arrival: Instant at Jomo Kenyatta Airport, Nairobi

Skyway Tip: Apply at least 7 days before travel to avoid delays.

Main Attractions and Things to Do in Kenya

1. Maasai Mara National Reserve

  • Experience thrilling safaris and witness the Great Migration.
  • Spot lions, elephants, cheetahs, and other wildlife in their natural habitat.

2. Nairobi

  • Visit Nairobi National Park, Giraffe Centre, and Karen Blixen Museum.
  • Explore vibrant markets and urban culture.

3. Amboseli National Park

  • Enjoy spectacular views of Mount Kilimanjaro.
  • Photograph large herds of elephants in open plains.

4. Mombasa

  • Relax on Diani and Nyali beaches.
  • Explore historic Fort Jesus and Old Town.

5. Lake Naivasha & Lake Turkana

  • Boat rides and birdwatching at Lake Naivasha.
  • Discover the unique landscapes of Lake Turkana.

Important Notes

  • Passport must be valid for at least 6 months from entry.
  • Carry a printed copy of your e-visa or supporting documents.
  • Ensure visa type matches travel purpose (tourist/business).

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. Can Indian citizens get a Kenya visa online?

Yes, e-visa applications are available for Indian travelers.

2. How long is a Kenya tourist visa valid?

Typically up to 90 days for single entry.

3. What documents are required?

Passport valid for at least 6 months, visa application form, photograph, and travel itinerary or hotel booking.

4. How long does visa processing take?

E-Visa: 3–5 working days; Visa on Arrival: immediate at Nairobi airport.

5. Can I extend my Kenya visa?

Yes, extensions may be granted through Kenyan immigration authorities under certain conditions.
